Prevention Checklist: Stay Safe Online

1. Do not click unknown links.
2. Always check website URL.
3. Never share OTP or passwords.
4. Enable two-factor authentication.
5. Use strong passwords.
6. Avoid public Wi-Fi for payments.
7. Update apps regularly.
8. Verify messages before trusting.
